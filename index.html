Workflow at a glance

methods
STEP 01

Build initial cluster

Size-specific magic-number geometries: 13·38·55 cuboctahedra, 75 Marks decahedron, 147 cuboctahedron. Compositions sweep x=0 to 1 with seed-fixed random alloy ordering.

STEP 02

Pure-Python well-tempered metaD

UMA-1p2-OMAT calculator drives ASE Langevin MD at size-dependent temperatures. SOAP-derived Q4, Q6, cn form the 3D collective-variable space; well-tempered Gaussians (γ=10) accumulate in HILLS.

STEP 03

Re-relax & rank

Low-FES frames (≤ FESmin+0.3 eV, 50 per system) are LBFGS-relaxed back to 0 K minima, time-averaged for finite-T mixing energy, and projected onto the lower convex hull to compute energy-above-hull.
